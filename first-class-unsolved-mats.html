Steps to Make a Sandwich

  1. Take two bread slices
  2. Use a knife to lather on a condiment of your choice on one or both slices of bread
  3. Place meat of your choice on a bread slice
  4. Place cheese of your choice on top of the meat
  5. Place another layer of meat on top of the cheese
  6. Stack your favorite vegetables on top of the second layer of meat
  7. Put the other slice of bread on top of the vegetables
  8. Congratulations! You made a sandwich.
